About the Brand

Eerie, Organic, Unforgettable

Maples Bone Yard is built around the balance of life and death, softness and edge, beauty and ruin. Every piece is designed to feel like more than merch — it should feel like a statement, a symbol, or something you carry.


The goal is simple: make artwork that feels deep, looks bold, and wears like it belongs to someone who has lived through something.
